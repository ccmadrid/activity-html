THAT KIDMADRID
Graphic Design

My name is Carl Christian Madrid, but I prefer to be called Christian. I have a background in Engineering Physics from Carleton University.I always had a partiality to the arts, but until recently, I had been skeptical of its validity as a stable career. Learning more about the harmony between design and tech is one of the factors that hooked me on design.

So far, my experience in the design school has been enjoyable and educating. It is a great asset to be able to use and learn professional techniques and software instead of teaching myself through trial, error, and Google.

The nerdiest thing I do that pertains to art and design is being a part of the designer vinyl toy phenomenon —I am a Kidrobot fanboy—, and more recently with the purchase of a dslr, being a shutterbug. Both hobbies are expensive, and as a student throwing away 3 years of one of the most expensive Engineering programs that Carleton offers, I'm pushed to be successful in this new career so that I can continue my designer addictions.
